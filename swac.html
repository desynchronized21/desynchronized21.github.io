Star Wars: Attack Of The Clones



Star Wars is back with episode 2 The attack of the clones. TIme has passed and the chosen one is now all grown up and strong. The Republic is threatened by the seperatist leader and former Jedi Master Count Dooku. Senator Padmé Amidala arrives at Coruscant to vote on a motion to create an army to assist the Jedi against the threat. Escaping her assassination by seconds, Senator Amidala is place under the protection of Jedi Master Obi-Wan Kenobi and his apprentice Anakin Skywalker. Both the Jedi foil the second attempt on Senator Amidala's life and subdue Zam Wesell, killed by her employer a bounty hunter before she can reveal his identity. Obi-Wan is tasked by the Jedi Council to find the bounty hunter and his padawan Anakin Skywalker is tasked to protect the Senator and safely escort her to Naboo, where the two fall in love in spite of the Jedi Code that forbids relationships.
Obi-Wan's investigation leads him to the mysterious ocean planet of Kamino,where a clone army is being produced under the order of a decesead Jedi Master, with bounty hunter Jango Fett serving as their genetic template. Obi-Wan arrives at the conclusion that Jango is the bounty hunter he is seeking, and after a brief battle is successful in placing a homing beacon on Jango's ship, the Slave I. Obi-Wan then follows Jango and his clone son, Boba, to the planet Geonosis. Meanwhile, Anakin is troubled by visions of his mother, Shmi, in pain and decides to return to his homeworld Tatooine with Padmé in order to save her. Watto reveals that he sold Shmi to moisture farmer Cliegg Lars, who then freed and married her. Cliegg informs Anakin that she was abducted by Tusken Raiders weeks earlier and is likely dead. Determined to find his mother, Anakin ventures out and finds her at the Tusken campsite, still barely alive. After she dies in his arms, an enraged Anakin massacres the tribe. He later declares to Padmé that he will find a way to prevent the deaths of those he loves.



On Geonosis, Obi-Wan discovers a Separatist gathering led by Count Dooku, who is developing a droid army with Trade Federation Viceroy Nute Gunray and ordered the attempts on Padmé's life. Obi-Wan sends his findings to the Jedi Council but is captured by the Separatist droids. Dooku meets Obi-Wan in his cell and explains his role in the Confederacy of Independent Systems' formation, while implying that a large portion of the Galactic Senate is corrupt and under the control of his new master, Darth Sidious. He then invites Obi-Wan to join him and stop Sidious. When Obi-Wan refuses, Dooku claims that his late master and Dooku's former apprentice Qui-Gon Jinn would have, had he been alive. Meanwhile, Senate Representative Jar Jar Binks proposes a successful vote to grant emergency powers to Chancellor Palpatine, allowing the clone army to be authorized.
Anakin and Padmé head to Geonosis to rescue Obi-Wan, but are captured by Jango. Dooku orders the trio to death, but they are saved by a battalion of clone troopers led by Yoda, Mace Windu, and other Jedi. Windu beheads Jango during the ensuing battle while Obi-Wan and Anakin intercept Dooku engaging in a lighsaber duel. Dooku injures Obi-Wan and severs Anakin's right arm, but Yoda intercepts and defends them. Dooku uses the Force to distract Yoda and escapes to Coruscant, where he delivers the plans of a super-weapon to Sidious. As the Jedi acknowledge the beginning of the Clone Wars, Anakin is fitted with a robotic hand and marries Padmé on Naboo, with C-3PO and R2-D2 as the only witnesses.
The episode 2 introduced us to Hayden Christensen as grown up Anakin Skywalker and this movie turnes the tide in the war between the Republic and the seperatist.
