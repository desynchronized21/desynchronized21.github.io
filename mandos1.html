Chapter 1: The Mandalorian



Five years have passed after the downfall of the Empire. A Mandalorian bounty hunter collects a bounty after a scuffle exchange in a bar on Maldo Kreis and returns to a bounty hunting guildhall on Nevarro in his ship, the Razor Crest. He meets with his guild's leader, Greef Karga, who mostly has low-paying bounties that will not cover travel expenses. In hopes for getting a higher bounty the mandalorian accpets a mysterios bounty and Karga can only give the address of the client as they want the bounty to be a private matter.
The Client had imperial stormtrooper as his bodyguards and gives the Mandalorian a vague target to bring back alive. The only information he is given that the target is 50 years old and his last known location. In exchange, The Client promises to reward the bounty hunter with a crate of Beskar, a rare metal used by Mandalorians to forge impenetrable Mandalorian armor. Receiving a single bar of Beskar as a down payment, the Mandalorian meets with the Armorer at an enclave housing fellow Mandalorians. The Armorer, who melts the beskar into a cauldron, says the metal was gathered in The Great Purge and the excess will sponsor other foundlings, like the Mandalorian once was.



The Mandalorian travels to the desert planet Arvala-7,native home of the Ugnaughts and meets a native named Kuiil who wants to be rid of the criminals and mercenaries who now inhabit the area to bring back peace in hi valley. Kuiil teaches the Mandalorian to ride a Blurrg, as there are no land speed vehicles to traverse the area, and sends him to where his bounty is located. Upon reaching the hideout, the Mandalorian grudgingly teams up with bounty droid IG-11. They manage to clear the entire facility of its Nikto guards and discover that the bounty is a green, big-eared infant creature. IG-11 plans to kill it, but the Mandalorian blasts the droid to protect the baby and bounty.




Chapter 2: The Child



On his way back to his ship Razor Crest the mandalorian is ambushed by a trio of Trandoshan warriors. He disintegrates one attempting to rush and kill the Child, revealing a tracking fob. When he reaches back to his ship he finds it all stripped of its parts and weapons by Jawas and the retreat in their sandcrawler after a small batlle. As they retreat back they stun the mandalorian with an ion blast.
With the help of Kuiil he soon finds the Jawas but he had to bargain for his parts by helping them get an egg of a mud-horn, a large horned beast.



The Mandalorian locates one of the mud horns den and gets beaten up by it. As the beast charges for his finishing move against the Mandalorian, his weapons failing and his armor heavily damaged, the mud horn suddenly stops and the mandalorian sees the creature flying, he sees that the child has stopped the creature somehow unknown to the force. Tired after using the force the child goes to sleep. This allows the Mandalorian to go for the kill. He returns with the Egg, and the Jawas cut it open and eat its contents. He and Kuiil repair his ship, after which Kuiil turns down the Mandalorian's offer of reward and crewing his ship. After parting as friends, the Mandalorian takes to space and the Child wakes for the first time after exhausting itself using the Force.
